A significant analytical undertaking geared entirely towards designing, centralizing, and deploying intuitive cloud-security visibility interfaces for stakeholder review and rapid organizational insight.

*   **Insight Discovery:** Established analytical views that consolidated cross-service reporting logs natively natively and enabled real-time actioning for operations engineering.

These dashboards are crucial visual aides relied upon for real-time risk evaluation and security hygiene compliance.
